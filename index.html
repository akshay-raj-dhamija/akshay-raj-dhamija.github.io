About Me

A computer whisperer who loves communicating with people as much as machines. My research is focused towards deep learning for computer vision and I have a keen interest in application of everyday research to real world scenarios. My prior experience is spread across various problem domains such as face recognition, object recognition and detection, metric learning, scaling and optimizing pre-existing code. Apart from research I love designing user facing products and share an enthusiasm for managing painless project deliveries, if such a world exists :).

You do not need to know everything, everything you need to know you will figure it out when you need to know it.
